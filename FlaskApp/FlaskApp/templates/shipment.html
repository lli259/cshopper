Shipping Policy



Orders must be placed by 12pm EST Monday – Friday to start processing on the same day. Processing time usually takes 1-2 business days. Delivery times are based on orders placed between Monday – Friday. C-Shopper offers FREE STANDARD 3 DAY SHIPPING on all U.S. merchandise orders. Our standard shipping option is Standard 3 Day shipping. 


For shipping to Hawaii & Alaska, P.O. Box and APO/FPO/DPO Military Addresses, C-Shopper uses USPS Priority. The delivery charge is based on the location. Additional charge may apply to orders shipping to these areas.


Packages do not ship from our distribution centers on the following holidays:

• New Year's Day

• Martin Luther King Jr. Day

• President's Day

• Memorial Day

• 4th of July

• Labor Day

• Thanksgiving

• Christmas
